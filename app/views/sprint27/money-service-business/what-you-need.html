For this section you may need to know:

  • which countries your branches or agents operate in
  • your Intermediary Payment Service Provider’s name
  • your Intermediary Payment Service Provider’s HM Revenue and Customs registration number
  • where you expect to send the largest amounts of money
  • where you expect to send the greatest number of money transfers

You'll also need to know:

  • how much throughput you expect in the next 12 months
  • how many transactions you expect in the next 12 months
  • if your systems can identify linked payments
  • if you transfer money without using formal banking systems
  • how many transactions you expect in the next 12 months
  • which currencies you expect to be most popular with customers
  • who will supply your foreign currency
