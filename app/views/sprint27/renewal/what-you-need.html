Money Service Businesses

If you're a Money Service Business you'll need to know:

  • your throughput
  • the number of money transfers you made
  • the countries you sent the largest amounts of money to
  • the countries you sent the most transactions to
  • the number of currency exchange transactions you made
  • the currencies you supplied the most
  • who supplied your foreign currency

High Value Dealers

If you're a High Value Dealer you'll need to know:

  • the proportion of your turnover from cash payments over €15,000 in value
  • details of cash payments over €15,000 in value that came from customers you've never met in person
