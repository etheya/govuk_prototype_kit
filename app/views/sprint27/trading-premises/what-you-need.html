What you need

This section is: Trading premises

{% include "includes/save-message.html" %}

For each of your trading premises, you’ll need to know:

  • the trading name and address
  • the date you’ll start trading from these premises
  • if it’s also a residential address
  • what the business does from these premises

Agents

If you use agents, we also need to know where your agents trade from. An agent is an individual or business that works on behalf of your business, to provide your services. An employee is not an agent.

You'll need the following information for each agent:

  • your agent's name
  • your agent's business structure

Depending on your agent's circumstances, you may also need:

  • your agent's company registration number
  • your agent's date of birth
