This section is: Trust or Company Service Provider

{% include "includes/save-message.html" %}

For this section you may need to know:

  • if you only sell off-the-shelf companies
  • if you create complex corporate structures for clients, or plan to do so in the future
  • the Money Laundering Regulation reference number of the Trust or Company Service Provider you use

You'll also need to know:

  • what kind of Trust or Company Service Provider you are
  • which services your business provides
  • whether or not you use the services of another Trust or Company Service Provider
