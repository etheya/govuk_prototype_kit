How much you have to pay

The amount you have to pay depends on whether or not you’ve paid your previous fees yet. If you’ve:

  • paid all of your fees so far - you need to pay the current amendment fee
  • not paid any of your previous fees - you need to pay the total from the itemised breakdown
  • paid some of your fees so far, but not all of them - you need to deduct what you’ve paid from the total of the itemised breakdown and pay what’s left

Your application won’t be reviewed by HM Revenue and Customs until all the fees are paid.
