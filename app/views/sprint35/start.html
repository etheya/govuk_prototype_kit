Manage your anti-money laundering supervision

Use this service to register with HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) for supervision under the Money Laundering Regulations.

If you’re already registered you can update your information, de-register or renew your registration.

Start now

Before you register

You’ll need to answer a few questions so HMRC can identify your business. You might need to look up a couple of things.

Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR) number

If we can’t find your business automatically, we’ll ask for your UTR number. You’ll find it in the header of any letter issued by HMRC. It may be labelled ‘Tax Reference’, ‘UTR’ or ‘Official Use’.

Company registration number

If your business is a limited company you’ll need this. You can search for your company registration number, or find it on official letters from Companies House or your Certificate of Incorporation.
